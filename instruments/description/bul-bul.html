Bul Bul Tarang

Origins and History:

The Bul Bul Tarang, also known as the Indian banjo, is a traditional string instrument popular in Indian and Pakistani folk music.

Instrument Features:

It consists of multiple strings and is played with a combination of keys and plucking techniques.

Musical Role:

The Bul Bul Tarang produces a melodious and rhythmic sound, widely used in folk and light classical music.

Conclusion

The Bul Bul Tarang continues to be cherished for its unique sound and ease of playability.
