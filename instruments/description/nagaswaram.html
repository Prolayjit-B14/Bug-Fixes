Nagaswaram

Origins and History:

The Nagaswaram is a traditional South Indian wind instrument used in classical Carnatic music and temple rituals.

Instrument Features:

It is a double-reed instrument made of wood, known for its powerful and melodious sound.

Musical Role:

The Nagaswaram is prominently played in religious ceremonies, weddings, and classical performances.

Conclusion

The Nagaswaram remains an essential part of South Indian music and cultural heritage.
