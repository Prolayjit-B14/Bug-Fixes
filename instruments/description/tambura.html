Tambura

Origins and History:

The Tambura is a traditional string instrument used in Indian classical music to provide a harmonic drone.

Instrument Features:

It has a long neck, four or five strings, and produces a rich, resonant sound that supports melodies.

Musical Role:

The Tambura is essential for maintaining tonal stability in Indian classical performances.

Conclusion

The Tambura continues to be a fundamental instrument in classical and devotional music.
