Tumbi

Origins and History:

The Tumbi is a traditional North Indian string instrument, widely used in Punjabi folk music.

Instrument Features:

It has a single string and a distinctive high-pitched sound, making it a staple in Bhangra music.

Musical Role:

The Tumbi's rhythmic and sharp tones define the energetic beats of Punjabi music.

Conclusion

The Tumbi remains an iconic instrument, celebrated for its vibrant and unique sound.
