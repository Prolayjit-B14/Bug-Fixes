Shruti Box

Origins and History:

The shruti box is a traditional drone instrument used in Indian classical and folk music to create a continuous harmonic background.

Instrument Features:

A simple wooden box with bellows, it produces a constant drone sound, often used as an accompaniment for vocal and instrumental performances.

Musical Role:

Provides a steady drone that enhances tonal stability in music and supports singers in maintaining pitch.

Conclusion

The shruti box remains a vital instrument in Indian classical and spiritual music traditions.
