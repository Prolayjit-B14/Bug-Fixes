Gogona

Origins and History:

The Gogona is a traditional jaw harp used in Assamese folk music.

Instrument Features:

It is a small bamboo instrument played by plucking a flexible reed with the fingers.

Musical Role:

The Gogona is a key component of Bihu music and is played by both men and women.

Conclusion

The Gogona remains an essential part of Assam's cultural heritage.
